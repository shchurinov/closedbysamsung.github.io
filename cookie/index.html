Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how "Closed by Samsung" ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect and process your data.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide information to website owners.

We also use similar technologies such as local storage, session storage, and web beacons (collectively referred to as "cookies" in this policy).

2. What types of cookies do we use?

We use the following categories of cookies:

Category Description Legal Basis
Strictly Necessary Essential for the website to function properly. Enable basic features like page navigation, security, and accessibility. You cannot opt out. Legitimate interest / Consent exemption (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f), 152-FZ Art. 2)
Performance / Analytics Collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website (e.g., page visits, error messages). Used to improve our site. Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a), 152-FZ Art. 9, CCPA)
Functionality Remember your preferences (e.g., language, region) to provide enhanced, personalized features. Consent
Targeting / Advertising We do not currently use advertising cookies. If implemented in the future, we will update this policy and obtain explicit consent. N/A

3. Specific cookies we use

A. Hosting cookies (GitHub Pages)

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. GitHub Pages may place strictly necessary cookies to ensure the proper functioning of the hosting service:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
_gh_sess GitHub session cookie for secure authentication and hosting functionality Session Necessary
logged_in Indicates whether you are logged into a GitHub account (if applicable) Session Necessary
user_session Maintains user session for GitHub services Session Necessary
dotcom_user Used for GitHub user identification Session Necessary

📌 Note: These cookies are essential for the hosting platform to operate and are set by GitHub. We do not control these cookies. For more information, please review GitHub's Privacy Policy.

B. Analytics cookies (Yandex Metrica)

We use Yandex Metrica, a web analytics service provided by Yandex LLC, to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Yandex Metrica uses the following cookies:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
_ym_uid Assigns a unique identifier to each user for analytics purposes 1 year Analytics (with consent)
_ym_d Stores the date of the user's first visit to the site 1 year Analytics (with consent)
_ym_isad Determines whether the user has ad blockers enabled 2 days Analytics (with consent)
_ym_visorc Enables session replay and heatmap functionality 30 minutes Analytics (with consent)
_ym_hostIndex Used for load balancing and optimizing analytics data collection Session Analytics (with consent)
_ym_metrika_enabled Indicates whether Yandex Metrica is enabled for the current session Session Analytics (with consent)
_ym_sync Used to synchronize visitor data with Yandex services 1 year Analytics (with consent)

Yandex Metrica collects aggregated data about page views, visitor geography, device types, and user interactions (e.g., clicks, scroll depth). This data is anonymized where possible and is used solely for improving our website.

⚠️ Important: Yandex Metrica cookies are only placed on your device if you give your explicit consent via our cookie banner. You can withdraw your consent at any time.

4. Your rights and choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have specific rights regarding cookies and personal data:

For EU residents (GDPR):

  • You have the right to give or withdraw your consent at any time.
  • You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data.
  • You can lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

For Russian residents (152-FZ):

  • You have the right to request information about the processing of your personal data.
  • You can request the deletion or correction of your data.
  • You may restrict processing that does not comply with Russian law.

For California residents (CCPA/CPRA):

  • You have the right to know what personal information we collect.
  • You have the right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data).
  • You have the right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

5. How to manage cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Find instructions for:
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Apple Safari
    • Microsoft Edge
  • Cookie banner: When you first visit our site, we display a cookie banner where you can accept or reject non-essential cookies (including Yandex Metrica).
  • Yandex Metrica opt-out: Use the official Yandex Metrica opt-out tool.
  • Do Not Track: Our website respects the "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser setting. If DNT is enabled, we will not place Yandex Metrica analytics cookies.

📌 Note: Since GitHub Pages cookies are necessary for hosting, you cannot disable them without affecting site functionality. However, GitHub does not use these cookies for tracking or marketing purposes.
